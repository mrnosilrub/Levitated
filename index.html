Who We Are

Founded in 2019 by MIT‑trained engineer Ronak Shah and acquired by Mervis Industries in August 2023, Levitated Metals operates a world‑class 10‑acre heavy‑media complex just north of Houston.

Our dual‑bath process unlocks up to 10 million lb of premium recycled metal every month—Twitch, Zebra heavies, and Zeppelin—feeding foundries across the U.S., Mexico, and export markets.

Backed by the scale and 90‑year track record of Mervis, we’re expanding capacity and layering in LIBS segregation to deliver the “alloys of the future” with unmatched consistency.

Dual‑Stage Heavy‑Media Technology

1

Magnesium & Low‑Value Al Removal

Zorba feeds a dense‑media bath (~1.9 SG). Encapsulated Mg fractions & lower‑grade aluminum float, enabling early rejection.

2

Al vs Heavy Split

Sinks pass to a 3.2 SG bath separating high‑density Cu/Brass/Stainless (Zebra) from clean aluminum (Twitch). Inline QC audits ensure chemistry spec on every lot.

Quality Assurance

Final gravity check, metal accounting, and handheld LIBS validation lock in magnesium ≤ 0.5 % and iron ≤ 0.2 %—well below downstream alloy limits.

Circularity in Action

Every metric ton of recycled aluminum saves up to 95 % energy vs primary production and prevents 7.6 t CO₂‑eq emissions. By closing the loop on auto‑shred residue, Levitated Metals advances the circular economy—one high‑purity billet at a time.
